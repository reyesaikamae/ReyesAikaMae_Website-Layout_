Exercise Reflection

In this exercise I learned about float, overflow, display property, height and width, position, and background images helped me understand how web layouts are organized and controlled. Float showed me how elements can sit beside each other, while overflow taught me how to manage content that does not fit inside the box. The display property helped me see how elements behave on page, and heigth and width made me realize the importance of proper sizing. Position gave me more control over where elements appear, background images added creativity and visual style.
